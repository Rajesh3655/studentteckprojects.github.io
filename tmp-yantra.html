Login To Apply
आवेदन करने के लिए लॉग इन करें

Note : 1)  Applicants must read the Notification & Online Instructions carefully to know the eligibility criteria and other requirement for filling "Online Application".
2)  Application submitted through On-Line does not imply that candidate has fulfilled all the criteria given in the Notification and Application is subject to subsequent scrutiny and the application can be rejected if found to be ineligible at any point of time.
3)  Please go through the instructions step-by-step patiently. Application Form will be opened at the completion of all the instructions
4)  Fields * Mandatory to be Filled.

Step 1:- 		Click on the “New Registration” Button below.
Step 2:- Fill up the basic details viz. Full Name, Father’s name, Mother's Name, Date of Birth, Mobile No, E-mail Address etc.,
Step 3:- Follow the instructions and complete the Registration process step-by-step for generating a Registration Id & Password for login.
Note Candidates who have already Registered but have lost / forgot their Registration Id / Password can click on "Forgot Registration ID / Password" Button and fill up the details to display their Registration ID / Password.
Step 4:- Login using the generated Registration Id & Password. Fill the one time contact details.
Step 5:- Click on the Apply Button of the post which you wish to apply to fill the respective application details like Educational Qualification, Persons With Disabilities (PWD) if any, Community Details , Preference of Departments etc., Upload Photograph with Signature and Upload required documents.
Note Preference tab is provided for the candidates :- While filling the application, candidate has to provide the order of preference for selection of each Trade. Also department wise preference for selection has to be provided.
Step 6:- You can modify any details in the application if required, else click on 'Final Submit' button to Make Fee Payment for the application & Generate Application Preview. Finally click on Application Preview to take the print-out of the ONLINE application.
Note Candidates will not be able to modify any details in their application after clicking on 'Final Submit' Button.
Step 7:- To ease the payment of fees, payment are made available now:- Online Payment

For Payment through Online, Click on Payment and pay the Prescribed fees as mentioned.
Note DD, Postal Order, Money Orders and any other modes of payment will not be accepted.
Step 8:- Click on Home Button to view the submitted Application (Applied). Else Click on Logout Button anytime to close the application at any stage.
